High Protein Naan recipe

Back to homepage

Ingredients:

  1. For the Naan Dough:
    1. 2 cups bread flour
    2. 1 cup low-fat plain cottage cheese
    3. 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
    4. 1/4 tsp salt (optional, since cottage cheese already has)
    5. 1/4 cup water or milk, divided + some more for brushing on top before grilling
  2. Optional Toppings:
    1. 1 green chili, minced
    2. 3–4 sprigs of cilantro/coriander leaves, minced
    3. 1–2 cloves of garlic, minced
    4. Butter or ghee, for finishing
  3. Special Equipment:
    1. Cast iron pan or skillet

Recipe:

  1. Preparing the Dough: In a medium mixing bowl, add the flour, cottage cheese, baking powder and salt (if using). Mix with a spatula or wooden spoon until it forms large lumps. Add half the amount of water, about 2 tablespoons and knead the dough until it comes together in a ball. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of water and knead for a few more minutes till it forms a soft ball of dough. Let it rest till you prepare the toppings.
  2. Preheat the Pan: Preheat the cast iron pan over medium heat for about 8-10 minutes till it gets very hot and you see light smoke off its surface.
  3. Rolling the Naans: Take a small bowl and fill it with some water. Dump the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for a few minutes until smooth. Roll it into a log and divide it into 6 equal pieces. Shape each piece into a ball.
  4. Roll Out the Dough: Using a small amount of flour to dust the surface and the rolling pin, roll each piece into a flat oval or circle, about 1/4″ thickness.
  5. Apply Water: Using your fingers or a pastry brush, apply a thin layer of water to the top side of the rolled out dough.
  6. Stovetop Grilling: Place the rolled dough over the preheated cast iron pan, with the top side with the layer of water down. Immediately press the herbs and garlic on the top side using a wooden spatula.
  7. First Side: Cook for about a minute, until you see large bubbles forming all over the top side.
  8. Flip Over the Flame: Carefully lift the pan and turn it upside down over the flame to directly grill the top side of the naan. Grill over the direct flame for about a minute till the top side is lightly charred.
  9. Finish & Serve: Once both sides are grilled, the naan should come off the pan very easily. Brush with butter and serve warm.
